Struggles with Inkscape

The one thing I did not enjoy about working with Inkscape is probably not their fault.

I, like many others thought the AppImage would be the most stable bet only to find out that it suffers from one fatal flaw. NO EXTENSION SUPPORT. This caused me to have to do some searching and sleuthing, to discover that other people are also having this problem.

Which meant “My big break” Sadly, nope. Reverted to an earlier version and the issue persisted.

Finally, I bit the bullet and decided to install the native package on arch. Thinking, I’ve got nothing left to lose… I was right! It worked perfectly and even told me that I was missing some libraries. I popped them into pacman and Voila! it was done. I could actually use the ‘generate on path’ extension.

I had just witnessed its wonders in the form of Royal Logos Inkscape 1.0 Basic Tutorial Class 3. Where he shows us how to make the Olympic logo, a ring of leaves and a thicket! My interest was piqued.

This has so many use cases. I can’t wait to make something cool with it.
