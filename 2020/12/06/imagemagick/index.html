ImageMagick cheatsheet

Cheatsheet

Change format

convert source.png destination.jpg

Change format batch

mogrify -format jpg *.png

Transparent

convert -resize 100% -transparent -strip -quality 90 input.png out.png

Grayscale

convert source.png -colorspace Gray destination.png

Invert colors:

convert input.png -channel RGB -negate output.png

rotate image (-90 degrees)

convert -rotate -90 input.png output.png

Resize

convert image.jpg -resize 1024x768 output_file.jpg

convert source.jpg -resize 75% destination.jpg

Quality

convert image.jpg -quality 75 output_file.jpg

Montage 1

montage image1.jpg image2.jpg image3.jpg image4.jpg output_montage.jpg

Montage 2

montage image1.jpg image2.jpg image3.jpg image4.jpg -geometry +2+2 output_montage.jpg

Montage tiled - Vertical collage of 2 images

montage -mode concatenate -tile 1x image1.jpg image2.jpg output_montage.jpg

Advance Montages https://opensource.com/article/17/9/imagemagick-viewing-images

Image to PDF

convert output_montage.jpg output_montage.pdf

Image to PDF (smaller file size)

convert -quality 60 *.jpg output.pdf

Merging PDF Files

gs -dBATCH -dNOPAUSE -q -sDEVICE=pdfwrite -sOutputFile=temp.pdf pdf1.pdf pdf2.pdf

PDF page to image

convert input.pdf[0] output.png

Text to image https://legacy.imagemagick.org/Usage/text/

convert -background lightblue -fill blue -font Candice -size 320x140 \
          caption:'This text is resized to best fill the space given.' \
          caption_filled.gif
          
convert -background white -fill black -font arial -size 100x100 -gravity center caption:'O' O.png

convert -background white -fill black -font arial -size 100x100 -gravity center caption:'X' X.png

Color to image https://legacy.imagemagick.org/Usage/canvas/

convert -size 100x100 xc:white test.png

convert -size 100x100 canvas:red test.png

Patterns

convert -size 60x60 pattern:checkerboard -auto-level
+level-colors red,blue pattern_color_checks.gif

convert -size 60x60 pattern:hexagons out.png

Command to create thumbnail:

convert -geometry 100x100 -quality 100 orgfilename.jpg thumbfilename.jpg

Command to croping an image:

convert orgfilename.jpg -crop 128Ã—128+50+50 cropped.jpg

Command to watermarking with text:

convert orgfilename.jpg -font Arial -pointsize 20 -draw "gravity south fill black text 0,12 'Watermark' fill white text 1,11 'Watermark' " wmark_text_drawn.jpg

Annotate image

$convert in.jpg -fill white -pointsize 20 -annotate +20+30 'Blahblah' out.jpg

Replacing the background colour of a transparent PNG

convert source.png -background '#ff3399' -flatten destination.png

Or overwrite all images in a directory:

mogrify -background 'dodgerblue' -flatten *.png

Rounded corners

convert input.png -alpha set -virtual-pixel transparent -channel A -blur 0x8 -threshold 50% +channel output.png

And you can batch round the corners of images using the following:

mogrify -alpha set -virtual-pixel transparent -channel A -blur 0x8 -threshold 50% +channel *.png
